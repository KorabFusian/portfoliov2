The bodyguard app

wayHOME

wayHOME is a bodyguard app that, once you activate it, will notify your contacts when you get home... or not (hopefully when you do though).

wayHOME uses your current position and itinerary to check if you are safely getting home. If so, when you do get to your destination, it opens your text messaging app to notify your emergency contacts. If it detects a problem with your pathing for an extended period of time, it sends them your current position. Perfect for getting home after a party, or for your children after school, or to keep your grandma unworried!

Techs used:

  • - Dart
  • - Flutter
